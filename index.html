About Me

I got my PhD from College of Information of the University of Maryland, College Park, advised by Prof. We Ai and Prof. Furong Huang. I obtained my B.S. in Data Science from University of Michigan and another B.S. in Electrical Computer Engineering in Shanghai Jiaotong University in 2020.

Research Interests

My research focuses on post-training large language models to enhance their robustness and reliability.

I am also interested in applied areas of natural language processing, particularly in computational social science.
